Sustainability

QS World University Rankings (Sustainability) 2025

Bahria University, a Federally Chartered University in Pakistan, has consistently demonstrated its commitment to academic excellence, research, and community development. The QS World University Rankings for Sustainability highlight the university's proactive approach toward addressing global challenges, such as environmental sustainability, social equity, and responsible governance. In the 2025 rankings, Bahria University achieved a global position of 683, ranking 4th in Pakistan and securing 3rd place among Federally Chartered Universities, reflecting its strong commitment to academic excellence and institutional growth.
